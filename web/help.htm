E-DAY Help Menu

Registering

On the index page, register by filling in the Register form with the appropriate information, and then click the Register button. You will be registered into our system and brought to the Customer home page, where you can start bidding.

Logging In

Log in by filling in your username and password. You will then be brought to the Customer home page if the credentials are valid.

Customer Home Page

On this page, the navbar allows you to visit your posted auctions and your submitted bids. You may also log out using the exit button on the right.

You may search for auctions based on their type, name, or seller

Creating Auctions

You may create auctions by filling in the Create Auction form on the Customer home page. Please fill in all fields, and fill in "0" for Reserve Price if you do not wish to set a Reserve Price.

Lastly, the Customer home page shows you your best selling items, and also item suggestions based on what you have most recently bid on.

Viewing Auctions

To view an auction, you must first navigate to an auction through 3 ways: Clicking "My Auctions", "My Bids" or searching for an auction. Click "View Auction" or "View Details" on the particular auction to bring up an Auction Information page, where you can then see full auction information and bid history. You may also bid on this page by submitting your bid.
